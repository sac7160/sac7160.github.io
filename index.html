About Me

I am a MS student at KAIST's HCI Tech Lab(Human-Centered Interactive Technologies Laboratory)

supervisor : Prof.Sangho Yoon

My research topic is Sensing & Haptic, and in particular, I want to develop technologies that will create immersive, natural, comfortable, and effective user experiences in various ubiquitous computing environments such as VR/AR, as well as on various mobile devices. Specifically, I'm interested in researching techniques that help a wide range of users in general-purpose situations, such as text entry, rather than targeting specific users and specific situations.
