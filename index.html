Changhyeon Park

I am a MS student in the HCI Tech Lab at KAIST advised by Sang Ho Yoon. My research topic is Sensing & Haptics, and in particular, I want to develop technologies that will create immersive, natural, comfortable, and effective user experiences in various ubiquitous computing environments such as VR/AR, as well as on various mobile devices. Recently, I’ve been interested in enabling high-level interactions such as pose estimation and activity recognition by processing sensing data acquired from various sensors using machine learning.
